What is uMuse?

Developed by Kiera Lesky, Abbie Seale, Jadyn Scott and Jackson Hendrix, uMuse makes finding local museum exhibits much more efficient.

Keeping track of traveling museum exhibits can be difficult to do, especially if you live somewhere with several local museums. There could be several different websites, magazines, newsletters, and advertisements for various exhibits visiting a nearby museum, but they can be impossible to keep track of, and it’s easy to miss interesting showcases. uMuse solves this problem by scanning the internet for all things museum exhibits. By taking advantage of available packages and plugins, uMuse can search through several different websites, collect exhibition information, and condense it all into one place.

Some of the Museums we feature

We work with local museums to consolidate their upcoming exhibit information, allowing for easy user consumption.

Sun Studio

Sun Studio is known worldwide as “The Birthplace of Rock’n’roll”. It is the discovery location of musical legends and genres of the 50’s from B.B. King and Elvis Presley to Johnny Cash and Jerry Lee Lewis; from Blues and Gospel to Country and Rock’n'roll.

Children's Museum of Memphis

The Children’s Museum of Memphis is an arts and education organization that inspires children to explore, imagine, and create – growing minds and filling them with endless possibilities.

National Civil Rights Museum

Noted as one of the nation's premier heritage and cultural museums, the National Civil Rights Museum in Memphis, Tennessee, is steadfast in its mission to share the culture and lessons from the American Civil Rights Movement and explore how this significant era continues to shape equality and freedom globally.
